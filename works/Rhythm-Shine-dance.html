Rhythm & Shine

Overview

Rhythm & Shine Dance Company is dedicated to offering you the best of ballroom, social dancing and latin dance instruction in the North Carolina Triangle Area. Their passion is music and dance and they want to share it with you! Whether you are a beginner or seasoned dancer, Rhythm & Shine offers quality dance instruction in a fun, down-to-earth atmosphere. I designed and coded a website for their dance studio, which focuses on their showcase classes, events and social events.

‍

Target Audience

  1. Social dancers eager for group classes and events.
  2. Couples or individuals seeking wedding dance training or workshops.
  3. Aspiring competitors aiming for certification or performance guidance.

‍

Goals

  1. Provide a clean, intuitive interface for exploring dance offerings.
  2. Highlight events and class types with a strong sense of community and energy.
  3. Support business growth through easy-class signups and information access..

Tech Stack

  1. HTML5 & CSS3: Creative and clean structure with modern responsive design.
  2. Bootstrap/jQuery: Timeline structure for the history of the dance studio, and on-click same page scroll activation for better page navigation.
  3. Widget implementation: Using Meetup, Ticketor, Patreon, Mailchimp, and Google Maps so that the site is easier for the small business owners to use

‍

Challenges

  • Content Synchronization: Embedding Meetup & Ticketor while maintaining visual consistency was essential.
  • Mobile Usability: Many users browse class/event info on phonesâ€”navigation had to be clear and responsive.
  • Multichannel Cohesion: Ensuring the site, Meetup, Ticketor, Patreon, and socials present a unified brand voice and messaging.
