Services

What I do.

Web Design & Development

I create responsive, user-friendly websites from concept to deployment. This includes wireframing, prototyping, frontend coding (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React), and integrating backend functionality using full-stack technologies. I ensure each site is visually compelling, SEO-optimized, and mobile-ready. Additional skills include: UI/UX design, responsive layouts, CMS integration, SEO best practices, accessibility compliance.

Multimedia & Graphic Design

I deliver high-impact visuals across digital and print mediums, including logos, advertisements, social media assets, infographics, brochures, and video production. You use Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, Figma, and more to tell stories visually and support brand identity. Includes: Branding, digital illustration, motion graphics, print design, photo/video editing, visual storytelling.

Marketing & Content Strategy Support

You support marketing campaigns by creating targeted content such as email templates, social media graphics, and promotional videos. You contribute to strategic decisions with competitor research, A/B testing insights, and CRM integration (Salesforce), while maintaining brand consistency across platforms. Includes: Email design, CRM integration, digital ads, content optimization, analytics-driven design.

Creative Storytelling & Narrative Design

You craft impactful narratives that elevate brands, products, and user experiences. With a background in film, digital art, and UX, you understand how to shape a story visually and emotionally—whether it's through a website's flow, a video campaign, or a brand's identity. Your storytelling connects with audiences, drives engagement, and brings clarity to complex ideas. Includes: Brand storytelling, UX narrative flow, scriptwriting, video scripting, campaign messaging, storyboarding, and content hierarchy.
